Bayesian-Gaussian-Classification

My team mate and I developed a program that applies univariate Gaussian class condition probability density. The goal was to design a 2-class minimum-error classifier (dichotomizer) to classify IRIS samples into Iris Setosa or Iris Versicolour with respect to featyre Sepal Width. In short, we classified flower features. The source code can be found on my Github.
