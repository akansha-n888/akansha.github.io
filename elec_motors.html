Electrical Schematics

Line Diagram

Power Diagram

Line Diagram

Line Diagrams show simplified connections of electrical components. The inital layout shows 3 motors that are activated and deactivated with separate push buttons. The buttons are connected to a contactor with relay and a circuit breaker as a safety measure against excess current, short circuits or signal failures. The components are connected to a 1-Phase Motor that turns on a power indicator when in use.

Power Diagram

Power Diagrams indicate the symbolic electrical components needed to implement the necessary wiring for the design. The contactor with relay labelled K1, K2, K3 consist of a relay coil and a relay contactor.
