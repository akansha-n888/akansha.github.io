Finite Element Analysis

What is this?

FEA is a necessary engineering step used to verify load impacts and understand foreseeable deformations. The simple model analysis a piece of sheet metal. The piece of metal experiences a load of 1000 lbf along the horizontal (x-axis). A total of 3 tests were conducted to identify the load results under specified boundary conditions. The sheet metal piece was 8" x 4" x 0.2".

Boundary Conditions

  • Constrained along x-direction (Horizontal)
  • Constrained along z-direction (Normal)
  • Constrained along rotation of y-axis
  • Point constrained along y-axis

Simulation Result Summary

Test Result Expectations Met?
Stress No bending stress along the horizontal Yes! No bending
Displacement No displacement in the Z direction Yes! Constrained in Z-direction
Strain Uniform strain without major deflections Yes! Minimal red areas
