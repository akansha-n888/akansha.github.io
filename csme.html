Engineering Case Competition

My team and I hosted a week long engineering case competition focused on developing solutions for a post-COVID reopening. The case brief encouraged students to apply their engineering skill sets in both a process and design. I led my team to develop a case brief, scoring rubric and managed logistics.

Python Workshop

I co-hosted a collaborative Python workshop with Ryerson's Robotics team. The workshop was an introduction to python basics and was supplemented with a GUI project. The project was to develop a unit converter using the skills learned in the workshop.

Let's Talk Women in Engineering Panel

The Let's Talk Women in Engineering Panel relied on a new structure to ensure an interative experience for both participants and panelists. Panelists were presented a prompt, voted on the prompt and were visually placed on a spectrum. Afterwards panelists were encouraged to discuss the prompt and express how they viewed the statement.
